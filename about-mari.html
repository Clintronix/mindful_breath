About Mari

I am not what happened to me, I am what I choose to become.
~C.G. Jung

Karen “Mari” Castle, Ph.D. is a seeker of ancient wisdom and truth. She received her Ph.D. in Wisdom Studies (2014) focusing on the mystery rites of the ancient Sumerian culture. Her Master degrees in both Transpersonal Psychology﻿ (2008) and Oriental Medicine (2004), along with 15 years experience facilitating Holotropic Breathwork, provide the foundation for her mindful approach to health and wholeness.

Mari is a Transformational Life Coach and a Breathwork Facilitator. She authored two books on the ancient goddess Inanna of the Sumerian culture to reveal secrets for human development. Since 2005, she has hosted Breathwork workshops to provide a powerful container for people to feel safe and relaxed. With 20+ years as an Acupuncturist, recently traveling world-wide, she now brings the energy and healing of experiential Shamanism to her workshops. Mari is passionate about helping others live happy, healthy and fulfilling lifestyles!
