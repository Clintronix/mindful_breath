About Mari

Karen “Mari” Castle, Ph.D. is a seeker of ancient wisdom and truth. She received her Ph.D. in Wisdom Studies with a focus on the mystery rites of the ancient Sumerian culture. Her Master degree in both Transpersonal Psychology﻿ and Oriental Medicine, and her 15 years experience with Holotropic Breathwork, are the foundation for her mindful approach to health and wholeness. She is passionate about helping others live happy, healthy and fulfilling lifestyles!

Mari is a Transforomal Coach, Breathwork facilitator and Author of two books on the ancient goddess Inanna and the Sumerian culture. Since 2005 she has facilitated Breathwork workshops bringing a gentle, yet powerful presence to help people feel safe and relaxed. She has a License in Acupuncture for 20 years and her eclectic background includes experiential Shamanism.
